Watch Party

An easier way to split the bill.

Introduction

As a student, it can be hard to pay for a streaming service subscription monthly. Many of my friends have turned to sharing their accounts with others. Password sharing for streaming services is not uncommon. A study found that about 17% of respondents borrowed a streaming account from someone else in 2019.

People try to lower their invidual cost by sharing the subscription with family or friends. My partner and I saw an opportunity to help improve the way we share subscriptions through making the bill splitting and subscription process easier.

Design Direction

Scope

To design a platform that makes bill splitting easier, allowing users to share streaming service subscriptions with ease.

Design Goals

  1. Save money for the user.
  2. Create a time efficient and convenient subscription experience.
    1. Automatic bill splitting.
    2. Quick user pairing.
  3. Be transparent. Allow user to feel privacy over their information.
